Tame Impala is the acclaimed psychedelic music project of Australian multi-instrumentalist, singer, and producer Kevin Parker. His music centers around introspective themes like loneliness, nostalgia, and heartbreak - produced by heavily produced synth layers, swirling guitars, and melodic vocals.
